What makes Aaifit pads different from other brands?

Aaifit pads revolutionize period care with our patented BioComfort‚Ñ¢ technology. Unlike conventional pads, we use:

  • 100% certified organic cotton top layer
  • Plant-based absorption core (bamboo + corn starch)
  • Breathable, hypoallergenic backing
  • Chlorine-free, fragrance-free, and dye-free materials

Are Aaifit pads biodegradable and eco-friendly?

Aaifit leads the industry in sustainable period products:

  • 95% biodegradable within 6 months in composting conditions
  • Plastic-free packaging made from recycled materials
  • Carbon-neutral manufacturing process
  • Partnered with reforestation programs - every purchase plants a tree

We're proud to be certified by the Global Organic Textile Standard (GOTS) and EcoCert.

Can Aaifit pads help with sensitive skin or allergies?

Absolutely! Aaifit pads are specially formulated for sensitive skin:

  • Dermatologist-tested and approved
  • pH-balanced to maintain your natural flora
  • No latex, perfumes, or common irritants
  • Breathable design reduces moisture and irritation

In clinical trials, 92% of participants with sensitive skin reported reduced irritation when switching to Aaifit.

How does Aaifit ensure product safety and quality?

Your safety is our top priority. We implement:

  • FDA-approved manufacturing facilities
  • Rigorous quality control at every production stage
  • Third-party laboratory testing for each batch
  • Sterilization using gamma radiation (no chemical sterilants)
  • Traceability from farm to finished product

All materials meet or exceed international safety standards for feminine hygiene products.
